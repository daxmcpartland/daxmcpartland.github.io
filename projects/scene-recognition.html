Deep Scene Recognition
  • Developed for my Fall 2023 Computer Vision class using PyTorch
  • Created two deep neural networks for scene recognition in images
    • Trained on the following categories:
    • Bedroom, Coast, Forest, Highway, Industrial, InsideCity, Kitchen, LivingRoom, Mountain, Office, OpenCountry, Store, Street, Suburb, TallBuilding

Custom Neural Network

  • 3 convolutional layers, 2 fully connected layers
  • Utilized data augmentation and other overfitting techniques
  • Achieved a testing accuracy around 60%

AlexNet Extension

  • Adapted the popular AlexNet architecture
  • Testing accuracy of around 88% (potential for higher results with additional training)
